# Stickers

Drop **WebP or PNG** sticker images into the emotion folders below. She sends a
matching sticker after some WhatsApp replies (probability grows with the
relationship stage: stranger 0% -> girlfriend ~25%; never two in a row).

- happy/  laughing/  shy/  love/  sad/
- miss_you/  good_morning/  good_night/  angry_cute/

512x512 WebP with transparency looks best on WhatsApp. Empty folders are fine -
she just won't send that kind yet.
